### **1.Common Egg:** ![](../../.gitbook/assets/Egg\_common.png)****

This is the typical and most used to spawn a Pet. This item can be purchased from the **In-Game Shop.** When a player open one **Common Egg**, it will hatched and spawn 1 **Pet** with a random rarity level from: **Common** up to <mark style="color:red;">**Mythical**</mark>  (Check this for spawn chances:[#2.1-common-egg](../../playposeidon/earn-mechanism/gacha-items.md#2.1-common-egg "mention"))

### <mark style="color:blue;">**2. Rare Egg:**</mark> ![](../../.gitbook/assets/Egg\_Rare.png)<mark style="color:blue;">****</mark>

This item can be purchased from the **In-Game Shop** but there is a limit of only 100 Rare Egg per day can be purchased by every one. When a player open one <mark style="color:blue;">**Rare Egg**</mark>, it will hatched and spawn 1 **Pet** with a random rarity level from: <mark style="color:blue;">**Rare**</mark> up to <mark style="color:yellow;">**Legendary**</mark>** **<mark style="color:red;">****</mark> No Common nor Mythical Pet will be spawned when open this item. (Check this for spawn chances: [#2.2-rare-egg](../../playposeidon/earn-mechanism/gacha-items.md#2.2-rare-egg "mention"))

### <mark style="color:yellow;">3. Legendary Egg:</mark> ![](../../.gitbook/assets/Egg\_Legendary.png)<mark style="color:yellow;"></mark>

This item is not available to purchased from the **In-Game Shop.** The only way to acquire it is by achieve monthly high score in most difficult **Stage** battle. Or if you are lucky, when open many Treasures at once, it may drop out this item. When a player open one <mark style="color:yellow;">**Legendary Egg**</mark>, it will hatched and spawn 1 **Pet** with a random rarity level from: <mark style="color:purple;">**Epic**</mark> up to <mark style="color:red;">**Mythical**</mark> (Check this for spawn chances: [#2.3-legendary-egg](../../playposeidon/earn-mechanism/gacha-items.md#2.3-legendary-egg "mention"))
