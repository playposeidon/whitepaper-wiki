# üèØ Chaos Castle Mode

This is a _Rougelike_ game mode where player can increase the power of your Hero and Pet when progressing through different stage and floor level. All stages in this mode are randomized stages (all monsters, obstacles, helps are randomized based on the Hero Level Power).&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (15).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### 1. Start a run in Chaos Castle&#x20;

To start a **Run** in a **Chaos Castle** you first need to select 3 NFT Pets then click \[Create Battle]

You first pet (left most) will be your primary pet and it will always fight along your hero in the last stage of each floor (4th, 8th, 12th). For the other stages, a random pet out of these 3 pets will be selected each time you join the battle.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (20).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Then you will be able to see a \[Fight] button. In each floor of Chaos Castle, you will need to defend a series of 4 stages with the 4th stage is alway a boss stage. Keep in mind that your Hero HP bar will carry over to the next stage. For example if you lost half of your HP in the 1st stage, when fight 2nd stage you will start with half your HP (instead of full).

As of now only the first floor is unlocked for player to challenge. Second floor (5th to 8th) and Third floor (9th to 12th) will be unlocked soon.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (13).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

During fighting monster in Chaos Castle, you will receive Chaos Essence. Once complete a battle before starting the next one you will be able to acquire one of the buff/curse/upgrade/item with the Chaos Essence you collected. This will help boosting the power of your Hero and Pet for the remaining battles.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (19).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

For potions, you will start the **Run** with 1 Minor HP, 1 Minor MP and 1 Minor SP. You can purchase more of these potion after each battle with Chaos Essence. Also during battle, you may be able to pick up airdrop potions that flying out from the base by hitting it with a Focus arrow. Keep in mind that these air dropped potion are only valid within a battle. If you don't use it, it will not be carry over to the next stage. &#x20;

### 2. Claim reward from a Chaos Castle Run&#x20;

Once you complete the 4th/8th/12th stage, you will be able to claim the reward of the Run. You can click the Claim button at the corresponding stage to claim the reward:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (16).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Reward of Chaos Castle Run including:

1. Hero EXP based on missing EXP to next LV
2. Common Treasure (Open to get many game items)
3. Chaos Gem (upgrade NFT Weapon Orb)
4. Good Luck Token (spin Fortuna wheel)&#x20;

#### 2.1 EXP Reward

The Hero EXP reward is given as a random value with a minimum is the sum of all EXP the hero received while fighting in the Chaos Castle and maximum value follow this table:

| Hero LV | Max % by missing EXP | Estimate max value EXP can receive |
| ------- | -------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| LV3     | 10%                  | 80 EXP                             |
| LV4     | 8%                   | 200 EXP                            |
| LV5     | 6%                   | 540 EXP                            |
| LV6     | 4%                   | 1000 EXP                           |
| LV7     | 3%                   | 1900 EXP                           |
| LV8     | 2%                   | 3300 EXP                           |
| LV9     | 1%                   | 4200 EXP                           |
