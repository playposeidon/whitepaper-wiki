These type of item is usually used for Treatment of a certain curse or disease

### &#x20;1. Elixir of Deep Sea

![Minor Elixir of Deep Sea](../../.gitbook/assets/Elixir\_level\_1.png) ![Light Elixir of Deep Sea](../../.gitbook/assets/Elixir\_level\_2.png) ![Greater Elixir of Deep Sea](../../.gitbook/assets/Elixir\_level\_3.png) ![Super Elixir of Deep Sea](../../.gitbook/assets/Elixir\_level\_4.png)

This item is used to treat the Curse of Poseidon. When used, the Hero Efficiency can get back to 100%. This item can only be acquired by playing the Fortuna mini game **Lucky Spin**. Details for this type of Elixir will be released in the incoming patch update.

To treat the curse of Poseidon, Player must use a corresponding Solution with the Hero Grade following this table:

| Solution and Elixir require                                                                                                        | Outcome                                                                                                                                                                               |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **A20: 3 **<mark style="color:yellow;">**Greater Elixir**</mark>** ** or **1 **<mark style="color:red;">**Super Elixir**</mark>    | Reset a **Hero **<mark style="color:yellow;">**Grade A**</mark> back to 100% _Efficiency_ plus have 20 days before the curse kick-in                                                  |
| **A8: 2 **<mark style="color:yellow;">**Greater Elixir**</mark>                                                                    | Reset a **Hero **<mark style="color:yellow;">**Grade A**</mark> back to 100% _Efficiency_ plus have 8 days before the curse kick-in                                                   |
| **A0: 1 **<mark style="color:yellow;">**Greater Elixir**</mark> or **5 **<mark style="color:purple;">**Light Elixir**</mark>       | Reset a **Hero **<mark style="color:yellow;">**Grade A**</mark> back to 100% _Efficiency_ plus with the curse effect starting immediately                                             |
| **B20: 3 **<mark style="color:purple;">**Light Elixir**</mark>** ** or **1 **<mark style="color:yellow;">**Greater Elixir**</mark> | Reset a **Hero **<mark style="color:yellow;">****</mark>** **<mark style="color:purple;">**Grade B**</mark> back to 100% _Efficiency_ plus have 20 days before the curse kick-in      |
| **B8: 2 **<mark style="color:purple;">**Light Elixir**</mark>                                                                      | Reset a **Hero **<mark style="color:yellow;">****</mark>** **<mark style="color:purple;">**Grade B**</mark> back to 100% _Efficiency_ plus have 8 days before the curse kick-in       |
| **B0: 1 **<mark style="color:purple;">**Light Elixir**</mark>** ** or **5 **<mark style="color:blue;">**Minor Elixir**</mark>      | Reset a **Hero **<mark style="color:yellow;">****</mark>** **<mark style="color:purple;">**Grade B**</mark> back to 100% _Efficiency_ plus with the curse effect starting immediately |
| **C20: 3 **<mark style="color:blue;">**Minor Elixir**</mark>** ** or **1 **<mark style="color:purple;">**Light Elixir**</mark>     | Reset a **Hero **<mark style="color:yellow;">****</mark>** **<mark style="color:green;">**Grade C**</mark> back to 100% _Efficiency_ plus have 20 days before the curse kick-in       |
| **C8: 2 **<mark style="color:blue;">**Minor Elixir**</mark>                                                                        | Reset a **Hero **<mark style="color:yellow;">****</mark>** **<mark style="color:green;">**Grade C**</mark> back to 100% _Efficiency_ plus have 20 days before the curse kick-in       |
| **C0: 1 **<mark style="color:blue;">**Minor Elixir**</mark>                                                                        | Reset a **Hero **<mark style="color:yellow;">****</mark>** **<mark style="color:green;">**Grade C**</mark> back to 100% _Efficiency_ plus with the curse effect starting immediately  |

Those Solution above can be used to treat Hero of any character regardless of **Hero LV**. With an exception is that only **Hero LV** greater or equal to **LV6** can be treated. For example:

You can't use **Elixir of Deep Sea** to treat Hero LV5 back to 100% _Efficiency_, Hero below LV6 must upgrade to next LV in-order to lift the curse. Your Hero will not be upgraded to next LV when use **Elixir of Deep Sea**\
You can use **3 **<mark style="color:yellow;">**Greater Elixir of Deep Sea**</mark> to treat a Hero <mark style="color:yellow;">**Grade A**</mark> LV6 or LV7 back to 100% _Efficiency_ with 20 days remaining. The elixir cost and effect will be the same for all Hero LV.&#x20;
