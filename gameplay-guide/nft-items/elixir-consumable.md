### &#x20;1. Elixir of Deep Sea

![Minor Elixir of Deep Sea](../../.gitbook/assets/Elixir\_level\_1.png) ![Light Elixir of Deep Sea](../../.gitbook/assets/Elixir\_level\_2.png) ![Greater Elixir of Deep Sea](../../.gitbook/assets/Elixir\_level\_3.png) ![Super Elixir of Deep Sea](../../.gitbook/assets/Elixir\_level\_4.png)

This item is used to treat the Curse of Poseidon. When used, the Hero Efficiency can get back to 100%. This item can only be acquired by playing the Fortuna mini game **Lucky Spin**. Details for this type of Elixir will be released in the incoming patch update.

To treat the curse of Poseidon, Player must use a corresponding Solution with the Hero Grade following this table:

Those Solution above can be used to treat Hero of any character regardless of **Hero LV**. With an exception is that only **Hero LV** greater or equal to **LV6** can be treated. For example:

You can't use **Elixir of Deep Sea** to treat Hero LV5 back to 100% _Efficiency_, Hero below LV6 must upgrade to next LV in-order to lift the curse. Your Hero will not be upgraded to next LV when use **Elixir of Deep Sea**\
You can use **3 **<mark style="color:yellow;">**Greater Elixir of Deep Sea**</mark> to treat a Hero <mark style="color:yellow;">**Grade A**</mark> LV6 or LV7 back to 100% _Efficiency_ with 20 days remaining. The elixir cost and effect will be the same for all Hero LV.&#x20;
