## Hero and Pet Selection

1. In the main screen, choose which hero you want to play.
2. The CAP represents how many PPP the hero can earn during the day. It will regenerate from 0 to full in 24 hours.

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/hero selection farm cap.png>)

3\. Click select Pet and choose which Pet to bring into battle.

4\. The Energy bar shows how much Energy your Pet has left. It cannot participate battle when runs out of energy. Like the farming CAP, it will recover fully from 0 in 24 hours.&#x20;

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/pet selection.png>)

## MAP SELECTION

1. From main screen, click Fight

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/main screen fight.png>)

2\. There are 6 stages in each map with increasing difficulty, choose a stage you want to battle.

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/map lv1 stage 1.png>)
