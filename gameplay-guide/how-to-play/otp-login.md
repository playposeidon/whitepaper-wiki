# ðŸ”‘ OTP Login



You can login to your mobile device and Window/MacOS version with OTP from your web version.

* First, login to your web version using method in [#login-to-playposeidon](metamask-setup-and-login.md#login-to-playposeidon "mention")
* In your Home screen, click the Setting in the lower right hand side then Create OTP.

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/pp homescreen.png>)

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/create otp.png>)

A random 6-digit number will appear and stay valid for 20 seconds.

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/OTP guide for mobile.png>)

Quickly open PlayPoseidon on your mobile/Window/MacOS and click Login OTP under Login button.

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/login otp.png>)

Input your 6-digit number into the box and click Login.

Now you can enter the world of PlayPoseidon !!!

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/success otp login.png>)
